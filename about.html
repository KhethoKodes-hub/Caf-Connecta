Welcome to Café Connecta

Café Connecta is a hotspot for breakfast and lunch meetings, conveniently situated at The Second Eye Resort on the N14 between Wrenichville and Kagung, Kuruman.

We have created a wonderful space to reach people and to connect with people. Because life is about connecting - connecting to people, emotions, yourself, things that make you happy and make you grow, things like a good cup of coffee, a better cup of coffee.

Our name "Connecta" represents our mission to connect people through the shared experience of excellent coffee, delicious food, and a welcoming atmosphere.

Our Mission

To spread positivity by serving happiness in every cup, one community at a time. We strive to create meaningful connections through the shared experience of quality coffee and genuine hospitality.

Our Vision

To establish Café Connecta as the preferred provider of the finest Specialty Coffees, while maintaining our uncompromising core values & principles. We aim to be the heart of our community, where connections flourish.

Our Values

Love & Passion

We pour our hearts into every cup we serve. Our passion for coffee is matched only by our love for bringing people together.

Quality Excellence

We never compromise on quality. From bean selection to brewing techniques, we maintain the highest standards in everything we do.

Community Connection

We believe in building strong communities. Our space is designed to foster connections, collaborations, and meaningful conversations.

Sustainability

We are committed to ethical sourcing and environmentally responsible practices. Our coffee is sustainably grown and ethically traded.

Innovation

We continuously explore new ways to enhance our offerings and services, staying ahead of trends while honoring coffee traditions.

Inclusivity

We welcome everyone. Our café is a space where all people can feel comfortable, respected, and valued.

Our Journey

2021

Café Connecta was established with a vision to create a space where coffee and business connect. We opened our doors with a simple mission: serve great coffee and bring people together.

Early 2022

Expanded our services to include wholesale coffee distribution across South Africa. We partnered with local roasters to bring the finest beans to businesses and homes nationwide.

Mid 2022

Introduced printing services to better serve our business customers. We became more than just a café - we became a complete business solution center.

2023

Launched our community outreach program, supporting local artists and entrepreneurs. We began hosting regular events to strengthen community ties.

Future Vision

Planning to open additional locations while maintaining our commitment to quality and community. We aim to become a recognized name in specialty coffee across Southern Africa.
