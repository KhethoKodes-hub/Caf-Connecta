About Café Connecta

Our Story

Café Connecta is a hotspot for breakfast and lunch meetings, conveniently situated at The Second Eye Resort on the N14 between Wrenichville and Kagung, Kuruman.

We have created a wonderful space to reach people and to connect with people. Because life is about connecting - connecting to people, emotions, yourself, things that make you happy and make you grow, things like a good cup of coffee, a better cup of coffee.
